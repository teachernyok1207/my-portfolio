My Travel Journal

My Travel Journal is a list of my domestic travels from 2022 up to present. It contains the features of the destination as well as it's location on Google Map. This webpage was developed in HTML, CSS, and React.

Live Link

Project Overview

"My Domestic Travel Journal" is more than just a static record; it's an immersive platform that captures the essence of my Philippine travels. The fusion of HTML and CSS creates a visually pleasing layout that reflects the beauty of the locales I've visited. With Javascript and React in the mix, the journal evolves into a dynamic canvas where I can not only chronicle my journeys but also interact with maps, galleries, and interactive elements that engage visitors. This synergy of technologies ensures that each travel entry is a unique storytelling experience, transporting readers into the heart of your explorations. From the pristine beaches to the lush mountains, "My Domestic Travel Journal" is your digital scrapbook of memories, inviting others to embark on a vicarious voyage through the enchanting Philippines.
